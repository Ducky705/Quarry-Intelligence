# Quarry Intelligence: Institutional Branding and Tone Guide

## 1. Core Identity
**Official Name:** Quarry Intelligence
**Primary Identity:** A professional, data-centric institutional analytics group.
**Objective:** Provide high-fidelity quantitative analysis for market-adjusted alphagenic outcomes.

## 2. Voice and Tone
*   **Tone:** Clinical, analytical, and professional.
*   **Voice:** Third-person, institutional "we" or passive voice when describing statistical results.
*   **Avoid:** Informal nicknames (e.g., "The Prism", "The Sniper"), technical buzzwords (e.g., "Quantum", "Vectorized" unless technically accurate), and gambling-heavy slang.
*   **Precision:** Use precise analytical terms (e.g., "Market Discrepancy" instead of "Good Bet").

## 3. Product Nomenclature
Models are organized into chronological **Analytical Series**:

| Series       | Codename | Status     | Primary Objective                          |
| :----------- | :------- | :--------- | :----------------------------------------- |
| **Series 1** | Pyrite   | Archived   | Legacy framework and historical baseline.  |
| **Series 2** | Diamond  | Nominal    | Core risk-adjusted precision engine.       |
| **Series 3** | Obsidian | Active     | Advanced ensemble architecture.            |
| **Series 4** | Quartz   | Flagship   | Institutional flagging and drift proxy.    |

## 4. Data Standards
*   **Source of Truth:** `docs/stats.json` is the definitive source for all performance metrics.
*   **Synchronization:** Any update to core performance metrics MUST be reflected in all documentation files simultaneously.
*   **Terminology:** Use "Net Alpha" or "Unit PnL" instead of "Profit". Use "Execution Environment" instead of "App".

## 5. Visual Guidelines
*   **Aesthetics:** High-contrast, industrial, and minimalist.
*   **Colors:** Series-specific accents (Gold for Series 1, Blue for Series 2, Purple for Series 3, Silver for Series 4).
*   **Icons:** Geometric, structural, and institutional.
